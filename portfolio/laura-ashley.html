Laura Ashley

See website

I started working at Laura Ashley in October 2015 as the lead Web Designer. Since then, I have worked on a wide range of tasks including:

Feature page design

Part of my role as the Web Designer was to design and build feature pages that would be displayed on lauraashley.com. These pages would advertise either a certain product, collection or a current trend in and display them in an attractive manner. I enjoy creating these pages as they allow me to display my creativity and really push my design skills to create something that looks great. Previous pages include, christmas 2016, t-shirt edit, wedding gift card and more.

Managing graphical assets

The main bulk of my responsibility at Laura Ashley is to manage and maintain the graphical look of the website and ensure that all of the relevant images are published and created ontime and are on-brand. This involved me working alongside a strict promotional schedule and I made sure I balanced my time effectively. Also, every graphic my team created, was also translated into French and German. This not only improved my knowledge of these languages but also how e-commerce differs in different countries.

Social Media content

I worked very closely with the Social Media team and I was responsible for some of the promotional assets they used on Laura Ashley’s Twitter, Facebook and Instagram. Not only did I produce content for to share but I also collaborated with them on new ideas on future posts.

Other responsibilities

  • SWF and GIF files for Affiliate Advertising Banners

  • Editing and updating Laura Ashley’s core HTML documents through their CMS

  • Updating PR Wordpress site
