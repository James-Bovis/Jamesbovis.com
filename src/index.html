A little about me.

23, Technology lover, Internet connoisseur.

Web Design and the Internet run through my veins. Whether it’s my current position as a Front-End Developer for PawSquad, listening to one of my Spotify playlists or posting my latest photograph to my followers on Instagram, I’m always online.

I’m pretty good at creating things for the web too. Some of these include self-teaching myself HTML & CSS, building my own Facebook Bot (Go and say ‘Hi’), competency in SASS, GIT and I’m a wiz inside the Adobe Creative Suite too.

While I’m offline, I love catching up on the latest blockbuster, brewing coffee, playing guitar and catching up with friends.
