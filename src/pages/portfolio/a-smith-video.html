A Smith Video is a client I worked with when I was first getting started with Web Development and was one of the first websites I made using Bootstrap. As the client was an aspiring filmmaker and YouTube producer, she needed a website in which would display her videos and also feature her photography.

I designed the one page site from scratch including the logo, layout and the color scheme. As it was a one page site, it didn’t take very long to build and came together quickly. By also using a great jQuery plugin, I was able to make a great interactive gallery which featured her photography.

Even though this isn’t the best site I’ve ever built, I still like the way it looks and it shows just how far I have come with my skills and techniques.
