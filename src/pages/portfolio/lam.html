Liquid Applied Membranes are a GRP Application specialist based in Kent and offer services such as:

  • GRP Flat Roofing for both commercial and domestic buildings

  • Resin Bonded Driveways

  • Cooling Tower Lining

  • Bund Lining

  • Water Feature and Swimming Pool waterproofing

During my employment at Suite Creative, I was assigned the task of creating some animated videos that would be used on their new website. The video's goal would be to better illustrate the companies services and the products that they offer.

The videos were put together using Adobe After Effects and the illustrations were drawn in Adobe Illustrator. What was great about using both of these tools together is that I could very effectively animate each individual layer and create something that was interesting to watch.
